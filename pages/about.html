About the Project

Introduction to E-Governance

E-governance uses digital platforms to deliver public services quickly, transparently, and efficiently.

Challenges Faced by Elderly Citizens

Small text, complex forms, low digital confidence, and fear of fraud can prevent meaningful service access.

Objectives

Design a simple, high-contrast, guided, and voice-enabled system for pension, health, documents, and payments.

Benefits

Improved digital inclusion, safer access to benefits, reduced dependency, and enhanced trust in online services.
